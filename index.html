Rock Paper Scissors

The Ad-Hoc design project "Face Off" is an artifact that focuses on changing the interaction of the game "rock-paper-scissors." The game table replaces the fist's pounding in the social system by separating the players and creating openings.

Simple Sprocket

I used this simple 3-D design of a sprocket to introduce me to multiple Rhino Functions such as "extrude curve," "create surface," "Polyline," etc.

Viewing Devices On Low Steps

Viewing devices on low steps of Columbia University. This is a concept of how humans look at each other and make judgments subconsciously in our minds. The design has an explicitly filtered view to show how we make conclusions before seeing people's character.

Concrete Patio

A plan view of a concrete patio in Seattle, Washington I worked on while working at Onyx L.L.C Construction. A plan view is essential when bidding on jobs. Knowing the dimensions of the area can help calculate the amount of rebar depending on its assembly protocol (12", 18", or 24" on center). The material cost determines the overall bid to hit the desired profit.
